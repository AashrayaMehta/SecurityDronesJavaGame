Instructions/1. Use the arrow keys to move the main character, James/2. You must travel from left to right in all security clearance levels avoiding security barriers such as patrol officers and their top of the line advanced systems/3. Clear the 3rd level to steal the Mazaedes spaceship/4. collect 1000 points by shooting the alien spaceships using the space bar, make sure to dodge the asteroids/5. If you are caught by any form of security or crushed by an asteroid, you lose a life and have to restart the level/6. If you lose all 3 lives, the game is over/7. Enjoy and Good Luck!
